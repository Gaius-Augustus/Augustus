Detailed Description

A summary of extrinsic evidence by source of evidence.

Example:
E : 7 (e1,e2,e3,e4,e5,e6,e7)
P : 2 (p1,p2)
T : 1 (t1)
numEvidence = 10. numSources = 3. srcnames = [E,P,T], freq= [7,2,1], groupnames[0]= {e1,e2,...,e7}
