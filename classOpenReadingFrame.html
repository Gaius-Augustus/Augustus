Detailed Description

Open Reading Frame (ORF), part of the exon model.

The reading frame of an exon is the position of the nucleotide following the exon in its codon starting counting at 0:
forward strand:
*** | *** index 0
*** * | ** *** index 1
*** ** | * *** index 2
reverse strand:
*** | *** index 2
*** * | ** *** index 1
*** ** | * *** index 0
